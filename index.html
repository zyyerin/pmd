Instructions

Create a digital measurement device that:

  • relect at least one property of something (anything) 
  • involve some randomness

Your app will have two parts:

  • the device itself 
  • a brief description of your project (what is being measured, how, etc.)

–– download starter kit

说明

创作的测量工具需要：

  • 至少反应被测事物的一个属性（被测物不限）
  • 有一些随机性

你的项目由两部分组成：

  • 工具本身 
  • 对你项目的简单描述（如，测量对象、方法等）
–– 下载初始文件

Distortion

失真

1. random()

Return a random number.

2. noise()

Generates a random sequence appears more natural than random.

3. sin(), cos()

Calculates the sine/cosine of an angle. Values are returned in the range -1 to 1.
